About Event

​​Begin your journey to become an Ethereum solo staker at ETH Taipei 2025!

​​(This event is tailored for newcomers without any technical background.)

​​​The Lido Community Lifeguards Initiative (Stakesaurus) brings this event series back to Taipei in 2025 together with AWS, Insight Software, and ETH Taipei following the Asia Blockchain Week & Taipei Blockchain Week editions last year.

​Join us for a solo staking workshop followed by a networking mixer in the heart of Taipei—Xin Yi District

​What you will get

  • ​​Be self-sufficient in running your own Ethereum validator node

  • ​​Dedicated AWS testing environment for practice

  • ​​Networking mixer with other aspiring solo stakers and participating projects

​​Participants can expect to go from zero to one on setting up their very own Ethereum validator on Testnet using AWS credits.

​​By combining the skills to run an Ethereum node and the features of the Lido Community Staking Module (CSM), participants will be able to enhance their ETH staking rewards by >2x (~7%) while reducing their required staking collateral to as low as 1.3 ETH per validator key (vs 32 ETH).

​​Supported by:

  • ​​AWS Taiwan

  • ​Insight Software

  • ​​ETHTaipei

  • ​​Lido Community Lifeguards Initiative

​
Stakesaurus，一位 Lido 貢獻者和 Home Staking Summit 的組織者，正在台北信義區中心舉辦獨立質押工作坊，隨後進行交流聚會。

​於 ETH Taipei 2025 開始您成為以太坊獨立質押者的旅程！

​(此活動專為沒有任何技術背景的新手量身定制)

​Stakesaurus，一位 Lido 社區救生員，在去年亞洲區塊鏈週和台北區塊鏈週活動後，於 2025 年將此系列活動重新帶回台北。 歡迎參加我們在台北市中心——信義區舉辦的獨立質押工作坊，隨後還有交流聚會。

​您將獲得什麼:

  • ​能夠獨立運作自己的以太坊驗證器節點

  • ​練習專用的AWS測試環境

  • ​與其他有志成為獨立質押者和參與項目進行交流

​參與者可以從零開始，使用 AWS 抵用碼 (Credits) 在測試網路上免費設定自己的以太坊驗證器。

​透過結合運行以太坊節點的技能和 Lido Community Staking Module (CSM) 的功能，參與者將能夠將其 ETH 質押獎勵提高 2 倍以上(~7%)，同時將其所需的質押抵押品降低至每個驗證者金鑰1.3 ETH（而非 32 ETH）。

  • ​AWS 台灣

  • ​Insight Software

  • ​ETH 台北

  • ​Lido 社區救生員計劃
